Colors

Few of my favourite colours. I intend to use variant of them mostly with navigations and menus. I will also refer to a color wheel to identify complementary colors. Mostly I will incorporate these primary rules and guidelines that surround color and its use in creating aesthetically pleasing visuals, thereby evoking a particular emotion, vibe, or aesthetic.

I want to also be more intentional in creating visuals that make an impact. Using primary colors as my parent colors, anchoring my design in a general color scheme.

I want to be able to use the color wheel which gives me opportunities to create brighter, lighter, softer, and darker colors by mixing white, black, and gray with the original colors.
